Flagship Product

Solar Fence Post

Each module is one ground screw, one post, one 150W bifacial solar panel. Panels slide between adjacent posts for seamless privacy. Looks like a premium black aluminum fence.

What's in each module

  • 1 helical ground screw 3" diameter, 36" deep. Driven with an impact driver in 3 minutes. No concrete, no digging.
  • 1 aluminum post 4-channel universal extrusion. Panel channels on all 4 faces — handles inline, corners, and T-junctions. One post does it all.
  • 1 bifacial solar panel 150W frameless glass-glass. Generates power from both sides. All-black, no visible grid lines. 25-year warranty.
  • 1 DC optimizer Per-panel power optimization and rapid shutdown. Monitors each module individually via the Groundswell app.

Installation

8 minutes. One tool. No contractor.

Each module installs in 8 minutes with a cordless impact driver. Panels self-align between posts. The rigid glass forces posts into perfect parallel — you can't get it wrong.

1

Drive the ground screw

Impact driver + adapter. 3 minutes. No concrete, no digging, no permits for the foundation. Call 811 first.

2

Slide on the post

Post slides over the screw stub. One bolt to lock it. Three height positions for leveling on uneven ground.

3

Slide in the panel

Panel drops into channels on this post and the previous post. The rigid glass self-aligns both posts. Maximum privacy, zero gaps.

4

Cap and connect

Snap on the post cap. Click the DC cable. Done. Repeat for the next module.

Built for shifting ground

Oversized panel channels with EPDM gaskets allow 16mm (5/8") of differential settlement between any two posts. If the ground shifts, the panels slide in their channels. Privacy stays intact. Power generation is unaffected.

Three bolt-hole positions on the post sleeve give you 40mm of height adjustment per post — so even on uneven terrain, your fence line stays level.
